# Maps

OilFlow2D produces oil-specific maps in addition to the standard hydraulic maps.

## Oil-specific map types

- **Oil-on-land time maps** — oil thickness on dry cells through time.
- **Oil-on-water time maps** — free-floating oil thickness through time.
- **Oil-on-water spatial maps** — spatial distribution snapshots.
- **Shoreline deposition maps** — cumulative oil deposited per shoreline segment.

## Standard hydraulic maps

Depth, velocity, WSE, max-value, time-to-depth — identical in concept to RiverFlow2D.
